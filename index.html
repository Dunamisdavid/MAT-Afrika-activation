What is the Activation Series?

A recurring series of free, practical, live online trainings — each one focused on one high-value skill. No heavy theory. No passive watching. Just real tools, real tasks, and real results you can use the same day.

What is the AI Automation Edition?

The first edition. Three consecutive days focused on one thing: showing you how to use AI tools and automation workflows to handle the repetitive tasks that eat your time — so you can focus on what actually matters.

Is this really free?

Yes. This is the seed phase of MAT Afrika. Our goal is to reach as many people as possible, demonstrate real value, and build a community of builders across Nigeria and Africa. Register. Show up. Build something.
